## 6. Version-Specific Notes

TallyPrime has evolved across releases, and a few shortcuts changed behavior or key combination along the way. Per the official documentation, take note of the following:

- **Filtering reports:**
  - **TallyPrime 3.0 and later** → `Ctrl+F` opens the filter.
  - **TallyPrime 2.1 or earlier** → `Alt+F12` opens the filter.
- **Filter Details (viewing where a filter match was found):**
  - **TallyPrime 7.0 and above** → `Ctrl+Alt+F`
  - **TallyPrime 6.2 and below** → `Alt+F`
- **Calculating balances with filtered vouchers:**
  - **TallyPrime 3.0 and later** → `Ctrl+F`, then `F7`
  - **TallyPrime 2.1 or earlier** → `Ctrl+F12`
- **Creating a party using GSTIN/UIN (or importing GSTIN/UIN from Excel):**
  - **TallyPrime 7.0 and above** → `Alt+L`
  - **TallyPrime 6.2 and below** → `Alt+F`
- **Report view (detailed/condensed):** Both `Alt+F1` and `Alt+F5` are documented as valid in current TallyPrime releases; in Tally.ERP 9 only `Alt+F1` was used.
- **Shut Company:** In Tally.ERP 9, `Alt+F1` closed a company. In TallyPrime, this was moved to `Ctrl+F3`.
- **SmartFind (TallyPrime 7.0 and later):** `Ctrl+F` also serves as a universal search ("SmartFind") to locate vouchers, masters, and reports from anywhere in the application, in addition to its report-filtering role above — the context (screen you're on) determines which behavior triggers.
- Many shortcuts that had **no Tally.ERP 9 equivalent** (e.g., `Alt+G` "Go To", `Ctrl+G` "Switch To", `Alt+K`, `Alt+Y`, `Alt+Z`, `Alt+O`, `Alt+M`, `Alt+P`, `Alt+E` as top-menu items) are new navigation paradigms introduced with TallyPrime's flatter menu structure.

### How to Discover Shortcuts In-App

- Press **`Ctrl+F1`** on any screen to open the context-sensitive Help topic.
- Press **`F12`** → General Configuration → enable **"Show all keys in the Button Bar"** to display shortcut key hints directly in the on-screen button bar.
